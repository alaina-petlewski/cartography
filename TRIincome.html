Toxic Release Inventory and Income in the San Francisco Bay Area

This map was produced as part of a lab for GSP 101 (Cal Poly Humboldt, Summer 2024) to address the question: are lower income communities exposed to more pollution? One mile radii were generated around locations included in the EPA's Toxics Release Inventory and the per capita income levels within these radii were analyzed. I concluded that proximity to these TRI sites was not equal across income levels.

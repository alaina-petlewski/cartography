Academic Interests & Goals

I attended Humboldt State University to earn a B.S. in botany with a minor in chemistry. I went on to earn a M.S. in plant biology from Cornell University. However, like many people, I re-evaluated many of my life and career goals during the ongoing pandemic and decided to change directions to pursue a career in Geospatial Information Systems. I am currently pusuing a certificate in GIS from Cal Poly Humboldt. I am especially interested in applying GIS to work in public health, environemntal conservation, or social justice settings.

Are Fresno County Public Library locations accessible across income levels?

This map was produced as part of my final project for GSP 101 (Cal Poly Humboldt, Summer 2024). I analyzed the per capita income of areas within a two mile walking distance around each FCPL location. I concluded that the FCPL locations do serve Fresno residents across income levels equally. Source: IPUMS NHGIS
